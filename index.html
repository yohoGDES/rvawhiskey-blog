Whiskey Holiday Buying Guide

One of our favorite things to do is to talk about whiskey and this time of year we are often asked for suggestions for gift buying. We decided to pull together a whiskey buying guide for this holiday season for any person / any price range. Trust us, we get that it can be totally intimidating to go to the liquor store and face the walls of bottles. Hopefully this list will give you a great option across a wide range of interests

Before we get started there are a few things to note about this list. Everything on this list is available right now in Virginia ABC stores. This is not a list comprised of the regular industry hitters which every whiskey nerd clamours for. These whiskies have been reviewed or tasted by us in the last year and are accessible to people interested in trying something a little different. I know we hate reading reviews and looking at lists of whiskey that are just unattainable in our state, so this list aims to avoid that.

There will be some classics, some curve balls, but primarily this list will give whoever you are buying for a great whiskey regardless of their level of interest. Let's jump in!

We have divided each section up to offer at least one whiskey from each type per price range.

  • Bourbon
  • Scotch
  • Japanese
  • Rye
  • Irish
  • Multi-Grain Blend
  • Other

$20 - 39 - Affordable & Available Must Haves

  • Bowman Brothers Small Batch (VA), 1792 Small Batch, Henry McKenna 10 Year, Eagle Rare
  • Sazerac, Crown Royal Northern Harvest, Blackback Honey Rye (VA)
  • Balcones Baby Blue Corn Whiskey
  • The Glenrothes Sherry Cask (or Bourbon Cask) Reserve Scotch
  • Hell Cat Maggie, Jameson Black Barrel Irish Whiskey

$40s - Step Your Game Up

  • John J Bowman (VA), Angel's Envy, 1792 Single Barrel (don't trust VA ABC, it's everywhere)
  • Reservoir Rye (375ml) (VA), Catoctin Creek Roundstone Rye (VA), Ragged Branch Rye (VA)
  • Highland Park 12 yr Vikings Honour, Ardbeg 10 yr

$50s - Mature Quality

  • Old Forrester 1920, Stagg Jr, Blanton's Single Barrel
  • Catoctin Creek 92 Proof (VA), Pikesville 110 Proof Rye
  • West Cork Barrel Proof Irish Whiskey
  • Talisker Storm, Glenfiddich 14 yr
  • Virginia Highland Whisky (VA), Stranahan Colorado Whiskey, Straight Edge (check the special order section)

$60 - 79 - The Sweet Spot

  • Booker's, Belle Meade Single Barrel
  • Whistle Pig 10
  • Oban 14 Yr
  • Hibiki Japanese Harmony, Nikka Coffey Grain
  • Bushmills Single Malt 12
  • Little Book

$80 - 99 - Most Impressive

  • Joseph Magnus Bourbon, Woodford Reserve Master Collection Smoked Cherry, Reservoir Bourbon (750ml) (VA)
  • Talisker Distiller's Edition
  • Yamazaki 12 Year
  • High West Rendezvous Rye
  • High West Campfire
  • Kavalan Classic Single Malt

$100+ - Topshelf Afficianado

  • Jefferson's Ocean Cask Strength
  • Lagavulin 16, Caol Ila 15 Year
  • Whistlepig Old World
  • Bushmills 21 Year Single Malt

If you're looking for something that falls out of the price range of this list please feel free to reach out to us on social media (instagram, facebook, twitter) and we will be happy to offer some recommendations!
